# 020 - Entity Model

**Document ID:** D-020

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Draft

**Depends On:** 010-Engine-Architecture

---

# Purpose

This specification defines every type of entity that exists within the Interactive Geopolitical Engine.

All persistent objects within the simulation must belong to one of these entity classes.

No simulation system may introduce a new entity type without updating this specification.

---

# Primary Entities

## Nation

A sovereign geopolitical actor controlled by either the player or AI.

---

## Leader

Represents the individual responsible for directing a nation.

---

## Government

Represents the governing structure of a nation.

---

## Military

Represents the armed forces of a nation.

---

## Economy

Represents the economic state of a nation.

---

## Diplomacy

Represents a nation's external relationships.

---

## Intelligence Agency

Represents intelligence gathering and covert operations.

---

## Technology

Represents scientific and industrial development.

---

## Population

Represents the civilian population of a nation.

---

## Infrastructure

Represents transport, utilities and strategic facilities.

---

## Resource

Represents strategic natural and manufactured resources.

---

## Event

Represents any temporary occurrence affecting the simulation.
